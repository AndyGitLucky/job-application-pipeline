About the role

The AI Research Division of Agile Robots is looking for a motivated and experienced (Senior) LLM / AI Engineer (m/f/d) Predictive Maintenance Agent. You will design and build AI agents that enable factory operators and engineers to analyze, understand, and optimize complex production systems using natural language. The role focuses on production-grade LLM-based systems that translate user intent into reliable data analysis, integrate with factory data pipelines, and support predictive intelligence use cases in real industrial environments.

Your Responsibilities

AI Agent Design:
Design and implement LLM-based agents that perform multi-step reasoning, tool invocation, and structured data analysis with reliability and determinism.

Data Analysis Automation:
Build systems that translate natural-language requests into complex analytical workflows over factory and production-line data.

System Integration:
Integrate AI agents with internal data collection, storage, and monitoring systems used in industrial production environments.

Production Readiness:
Ensure AI systems are testable, observable, and maintainable through robust evaluation, monitoring, and versioning practices.

Predictive Intelligence:
Support predictive maintenance and anomaly detection use cases by enabling data-driven analysis and model interaction through AI agents.

Deployment Collaboration:
Work closely with other teams during on-site deployments to validate, refine, and operate AI systems under real-world constraints.

About us
